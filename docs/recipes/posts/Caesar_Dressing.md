# Caesar Dressing


## Ingredients
* 1 heaping tbls anchovy paste
* 1/2 c parm
* 1tbls Dijon
* 1tbls lemon juice
* 1 tsp Worcestershire 
* 1/2 tsp sugar
* 1 1/4 c olive oil


## Steps
Add all ingredients except olive oil. Mix together. Then slowly wisk in olive oil till it's throughly mixed. Add several good grinds of pepper.
